   - No checked allowed official page explicitly reconciles these names as one exact same-start same-end canonical route.

2. `distanceKm` is not directly supported by one exact official route page with the rest of the required fields.
   - Miaoli County publishes only `火炎山登山步道全長約6.4公里左右`.
   - Another Miaoli County incident page separately references `火炎山3公里處` and `垃圾場登山口`, but does not define the full route boundary.
   - The allowed official sources do not publish segment-by-segment official distances that clearly reconstruct one exact route.

3. `durationMinutes` is not published by the checked allowed official sources.
   - No checked allowed official page gives an exact minute value.
   - Under the task rules, minutes cannot be inferred from general popularity, terrain wording, or non-official hiking convention.

4. `difficulty` is not directly supportable as the catalog's numeric field.
   - Miaoli County only says `攀爬難度不算高`.
   - Other checked official pages provide no numeric official difficulty level for this route.
   - That qualitative wording cannot be converted into the required numeric field.

5. `checkpoints` cannot be assigned as one exact official list for a single route boundary.
   - Official mentions found include `登山口`, `垃圾場登山口`, and `火炎山3公里處`.
   - These are partial access or incident references, not one complete ordered checkpoint list.
   - No checked allowed official page publishes a full same-start same-end checkpoint sequence.

6. `permitNotes` is not directly published for the exact public hiking route.
   - Checked official pages do establish nearby or overlapping protected-area context through `火炎山自然保留區`.
   - But none of the checked allowed official pages states the exact permit rule for the public hiking route boundary represented by `火炎山` / `火炎山登山步道` / `火炎山南鞍古道`.
