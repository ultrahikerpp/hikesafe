# Single-route alternate-source reassessment: 坑內坑森林步道

## Scope

Assessed only the exact canonical name `坑內坑森林步道`, using additional official municipal and tourism sources. No catalog record or Hundred Peak record was added.

## Rejected

The additional sources do not establish one coherent route boundary with an exact duration, numeric difficulty, and one matching checkpoint set:

- The Forestry and Nature Conservation Agency page lists the route as 2.11 km, difficulty 1, elevation 140–380 m, no entry permit, linear and bidirectional, with a suggested duration of “half a day”. Its detailed checkpoints total 1.9 km and 45 minutes: 0.6 km/10 minutes, 1.0 km/30 minutes, and 0.3 km/5 minutes.
- The Changhua County tourism page describes the connected industrial-road section as about 1.9 km, but separately lists a 0.32 km trail section and 0.2 hours. It also uses different endpoints from the Forestry detailed route.
- The Nantou County tourism page describes an approximately 2 km one-way route and approximately 50 minutes in visitor-contributed content, but that does not reconcile the official 2.11 km/half-day record with the 1.9 km detailed boundary.

Because the official values describe different scopes and no exact full-route duration is published for one selected boundary, adding a route would require inferring or mixing boundaries. No route was added.
